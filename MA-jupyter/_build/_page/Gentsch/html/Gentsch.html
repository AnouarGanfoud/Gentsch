summary(sum.lm)$test$pvalue


glht.table <- function(x) {
  pq <- summary(x)$test
  mtests <- cbind(pq$coefficients, pq$sigma, pq$tstat, pq$pvalues)
  colnames(mtests) <- c("Estimate", "Std Error", "z value", "p value")
  return(mtests)
  }

df.summary <- data.frame(glht.table(sum.lm))

NEE IS LINKED TO PLANT DIVERSITY¶

… The NEE in our study showed a remarkably strong negative gradient from mustard to mix 4 to mix 12 (Fig. 1), which suggested higher photosynthetic \(CO_2\)-C fixation rates with increasing catch crop diversity.
